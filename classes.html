0–5 min

Everyone joins the video call. Michael checks in, answers any questions from last time, and sets up what you'll cover today.

5–20 min

Open questions, tips for practising at home, and then the TakeawAI — one clear thing you'll remember and use from today's class.

Use Perplexity to research anything in depth. Create original music with Suno. Read a holiday destination through your phone camera live. AI for curiosity, creativity and fun.

Class 4

Build Your Personal AI Helper

Create a useful spreadsheet tool in Claude. Set up a personal AI assistant for something you do every day. Understand what's safe to share — and what isn't. Leave in control.

The TakeawAI: "You don't need to be technical. You just need to know what to ask."

Class 5

Make AI a Daily Habit

Bring it all together. Build a personal AI routine that fits your week — work emails, family logistics, hobbies, big decisions. Plus how to keep up as the tools keep changing, without it taking over your life.

The TakeawAI: "It's not about doing more — it's about doing the same things, faster and better."
