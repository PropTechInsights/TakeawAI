Why TakeawAI exists

Because AI is moving fast, and it shouldn't leave you behind.

Hi, I'm Michael

I started my career as a Teach First teacher — explaining complicated things to people who'd been told they wouldn't get it. That instinct has stayed with me through everything since: founding a tech startup, leading product teams at a FTSE 20 company, and now helping businesses build AI-powered tools through my consultancy, PropTech Insights.

I work with AI every day. I've seen what it can do — and I've also seen how alienating the conversation around it has become. Every week there's a new tool, a new headline, a new thing you're supposedly meant to know about. If you didn't grow up surrounded by technology, it's easy to feel like the world is moving on without you.

I kept having the same conversation with smart, capable people — friends, family, colleagues — all feeling left behind. I watched them go from curious to overwhelmed to just... switching off. And that didn't sit right with me.

So I decided to do what I've always done: make it accessible. TakeawAI is small, friendly classes and a community where you can make sense of AI at your own pace. I'm not here to turn you into a tech expert. I just want to give you enough understanding to use AI meaningfully — whether that's saving time at work, helping with a personal project, or keeping up with what everyone's talking about.

Every lesson ends the same way: "So here's the TakeawAI" — one clear thing you can take away and actually use.

How I do things

I meet you where you are

No judgement about what you don't know yet. No assumptions. I explain things from the ground up in a way that actually makes sense.

Everything is practical

Everything I teach, you can use the same day. No theory for theory's sake. Just real tools and real strategies you can actually apply.

I keep up so you can keep up

AI changes fast. I stay on top of it so you don't have to. Through PropTech Insights, I work with AI daily — helping global businesses and startups build AI-powered systems. I'm not teaching from theory. I'm teaching from practice.

This is just the beginning

TakeawAI is new, and I'm building it with the people who join early. If you want to learn AI in a way that actually sticks — come be part of it.
